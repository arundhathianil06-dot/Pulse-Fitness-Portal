Fundamental Healthy Eating Habits

  1. Prioritize Whole Foods: Base the majority of your meals on single-ingredient foods that are nutrient-dense and free of heavy processing.
  2. Stay Hydrated Daily: Aim to drink at least 3-4 liters of water every day to optimize metabolic function and muscle recovery.
  3. Hit Your Protein Target: Consume adequate protein with every meal to rebuild torn muscle fibers and stay satiated longer.
  4. Practice Mindful Eating: Pay attention to your body's true hunger cues instead of eating out of boredom or emotional stress.
